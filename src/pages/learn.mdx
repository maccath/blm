## Podcasts

- [The Good Ancestor Podcast](http://laylafsaad.com/good-ancestor-podcast) - An interview series with change-makers & culture-shapers exploring what it means to be a good ancestor. 
- [About Race with Reni Eddo-Lodge](https://www.aboutracepodcast.com/) - Featuring key voices from the last few decades of anti-racist activism, About Race with Reni Eddo-Lodge looks at the recent history that lead to the politics of today.
- [Hope & Hard Pills](https://hope-hard-pills.simplecast.com/) - Exploring practical insight on racial justice and social change.
- [No White Saviors](https://nowhitesaviors.org/what-we-do/podcast/) - Our collective experience in the development & the aid sector has led us to a deep commitment to seeing things change in a more equitable & anti-racist direction.

## Campaigns &amp; Resources

### [8 To Abolition](https://www.8toabolition.com/)

>The end goal of these reforms is not to create better, friendlier, or more 
>community-oriented police or prisons. Instead, we hope to build toward a 
>society without police or prisons, where communities are equipped to provide 
>for their safety and wellbeing.

### [Transform Harm](https://transformharm.org/)

>TransformHarm.org is a resource hub about ending violence. It offers an 
>introduction to transformative justice. Created by Mariame Kaba and designed 
>by Lu Design Studio, the site includes selected articles, audio-visual 
>resources, curricula, and more.
