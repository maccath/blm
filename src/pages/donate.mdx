# Donate

There are many ways to use our financial privilege to combat the current
injustices faced by Black people in America, as well as dismantling white
supremacy and race inequality at home.

**[Campaign Zero](https://www.joincampaignzero.org/)** - to limit police 
 interventions, improve community interactions, and ensure accountability;
 leading to fewer deaths at the hands of US police.

**[Bail Funds](https://secure.actblue.com/donate/bail_funds_george_floyd)** - 
 to secure the release of nonviolent protestors placed in jail pre-trial, 
 to get them fed, and to a place of safety during the US protests.

**[Black Lives Matter](https://blacklivesmatter.com)** and
 **[UK BLM](https://www.gofundme.com/f/ukblm-fund)** - to fight for Freedom,
 Liberation and Justice.

**[Runnymede](https://www.runnymedetrust.org)** - to fund an independent think
 tank challenging race inequality in Britain.

**[Exist Loudly](https://www.gofundme.com/f/exist-loudly-fund-to-support-queer-black-yp)** - 
 to support queer Black young people.

**[Black Minds Matter](https://www.gofundme.com/f/black-minds-matter-uk)** - 
 to help Black people access appropriate mental health services in the UK.

**[UK Black Pride](https://www.ukblackpride.org.uk)** - to celebrate African,
 Asian, Middle Eastern, Latin American and Caribbean-heritage LGBTQ people.

**[The Black Curriculum](https://www.theblackcurriculum.com)** - to address 
 the lack of Black British history in the UK Curriculum.
